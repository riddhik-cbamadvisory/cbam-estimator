What this estimate does not show: This tool uses EU Commission default emission values by CN code and production route. Your actual liability depends on your plant's specific fuel mix, process emissions, yield losses, and whether you have a verified monitoring plan under Annex III. For BF-BOF plants, blast furnace gas recovery and coke oven gas reuse significantly affect the verified SEE. For EAF plants, scrap quality and electricity source are the dominant variables. A full Annex III verified calculation can reduce this estimate by 30–65%. Scope 2 electricity liability is excluded from 2026 declarations but phases in later. This is not a compliance tool.
